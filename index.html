Why conserve water?

It is important because conserving water supplies us with more water for longer use and water has become necesarry because these natural resources are reducing along with the population increasing. Conserving water also saves energy. Energy is needed to filter, heat and pump water into your home so reducing water usage also reduces your carbon footprint.

Preserving water is most important because fresh, clean water is a limited resource, as well as a costly one.
