To conserve water

Small habits as an invidual to conserve water

Small habits can help you conserve water, even it is not a lot, the water you saves adds up over time meaning that you can conserve a lot of water by just implementing these small habits. It is also easy to get used to these habits. You can waste a lot of water even without knowing sometimes, so changing your habits and trying to conserve water is really helpful.

1
Turning off the tap while brushing your teeth can prevent water from being wasted. Brushing your teeth takes at least a round 2 mins, if you open the tap during that time, a lot of water will be wasted.
2
Fix your leaking taps and toilets, they can waste thousands of liters per year. Although it doesn't seem much, it uses up a lot of water gradually.
3
Try to take shorter showers if possible. People like to shower for a long time and it is hard for them to take shorter showers but it can reduce the water usage especially if you take a shower for a long time.
4
Collect rain water to do things like watering plants or outdoor cleaning, since those things do not require clean water, you can conserve water by collecting rain water to do things like that.
