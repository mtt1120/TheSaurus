Why

Why should we conserve water?

Water is an important resource for humans and all other living animals and plants, so without it, we will all die. Water is also needed for agriculture which gives us vegetables and fruits which is crucial for us to be healthy. Saving water also makes sure that our future generations and all the people on earth has acces to clean and safe water since in many parts of the world, people don't have access to clean water and are facing water shortages.

Reasons

Limited supply

Only 1% of the water on earth is fresh and accessible to us, so we should not keep wasting water because if we use water more that it can be replenished, we can face water shortages since it is very limited and we should be mindful about how we use it

Pollution

Using less water means less pollution. Dirty water from home, factories and other places ends up in rivers, lakes and the sea, and that dirty water contains lots of harmful substances and bacteria, and those things can be harmful to marine living species and plants.

Innovation

The need to conserve water push us to develop advanced new technologies and brilliant solutions. Some companies have even managed to make waterless products like waterless laundry machine, waterless shampoo and leak detection systems to prevent wasting water as much as possible.
