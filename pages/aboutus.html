Why we created this website

We created this website to raise awareness about the importance of water conservation. Our goal is to provide simple tips, helpful resources, and real-world solutions that empower individuals and communities to protect our planets most valuable resource, water. Every drop counts, and together, we can make a difference.

Our Mission

With this website, we aim to make more people in Myanmar aware of the water scarcity being faced in multiple areas and how they can manage to conserve their own water. Our goal is to provide a fun and engaging way of learning water conservation to build a more sustainable future. Through our explanations, actionable tips, and interactive games, we hope to inspire individuals to act!

Our vision

We envision to create a Myanmar where each community understands the value of water and conserves it for the future. Armed with the knowledge of being more effient in water usage as well as sustaining it in case of emergencies. In the future, may water scarcity be no longer a threat.
